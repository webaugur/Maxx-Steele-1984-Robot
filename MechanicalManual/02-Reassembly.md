# Chapter 2 — Reassembly

Reverse the disassembly sequence. **Do not rely on screws to pull the case halves together** — align and press the shell closed first.

---

## Close the main case

1. Place Maxx on his **front** and carefully position the **back shell**, aligning all screw holes.
2. Press gently on Maxx's **"armpits"** so they clear the **"shoulders"**. Press the case halves together **before** installing screws.
3. Reinstall the **fourteen screws** holding the case back to the front using the **thread-engagement technique**:
   - Insert one screw into its hole.
   - Turn it **counterclockwise** until the threads engage (you will feel the screw move out, then suddenly drop in with a slight thump).
   - Repeat the counterclockwise start until you are confident the threads are engaged.
   - Tighten only until the screw **just resists** further turning — this helps avoid stripped threads.

---

## Reinstall the head / remote holder

4. Reinstall the three **"eyes"** and the **foam pad** in the front plate on the top of the head.
5. Place the **remote control holder** in position, then install the front plate and its securing screw.

---

## Reinstall stabilizers

6. Replace the **rear stabilizer**: slide it onto the body, press down and forward until the under-body fingers engage, then install the two screws.
7. Turn Maxx onto his back and repeat step 6 for the **front stabilizer**.

---

## Finish

8. Stand Maxx upright and install the **battery**.
